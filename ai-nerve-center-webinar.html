FAQ

Quick answers.

Is the session free?
Yes. There is no cost to attend, and the replay is sent to all registrants whether or not you make it live.
Can I send a colleague the replay if I can't make it?
Yes. We'll email you a replay link the same afternoon. You're welcome to forward it. Anyone watching the replay can also book a follow-up demo through the link in that email.
I'm in EMEA. Is there a session in a more convenient time zone?
Yes. We're running an EMEA-friendly encore on Tuesday, May 26 at 9:00 AM ET (2:00 PM BST / 3:00 PM CET). When you register here we'll offer the encore option in the confirmation.
How much of the session is product demo vs. talking heads?
Roughly 27 of the 60 minutes is live product demo on a real tenant, walking through inventory, risk, policy, evidence, and audit trail. The opening 8 minutes is context. The closing 15 is Q&A. The 10-minute panel in the middle with customers, partners, and industry experts is a conversation rather than a slide deck.
Do I need to be a current Uno customer to attend?
No. The session is open to anyone evaluating AI governance, building an AI risk program, or curious about an integrated-fabric approach. Many attendees come from teams who use other GRC platforms today.
Will there be a chance to ask questions privately?
Yes. The Q&A panel takes both public questions and private ones routed only to our team. If you want a follow-up working session after the webinar, the post-event email will include a calendar link.
