AI Agents

Autonomous
intelligence

State-of-the-art AI agents that don't just assist - they act. From evidence collection to risk quantification, Uno's agents handle the heavy lifting so your team can focus on strategy.

Evidence Collection Agent

Automatically gathers, validates, and maps evidence to control requirements across every framework. Integrates with your existing tools - cloud providers, ticketing systems, HR platforms, and more.

Risk Quantification Agent
