The Uno Story

Mission to build an Autonomous Future!

Shashank Tiwari

Uno was born out of the quest to build a thinking machine that could automate away enterprise tasks, especially mundane and time consuming ones, that humans otherwise do.

Uno is the brainchild of two seasoned silicon valley entrepreneurs and deep tech product builders, Shashank Tiwari and Murugesan (Muru) Guruswamy.

Shashank has been an early contributor & executive across multiple successful startups (Nutanix, Elementum, Medallia, StackRox). Muru has been a driving force behind the conceptualization and emergence of real-time and distributed ML & AI systems with experience across innovative companies (TIBCO, Elementum, Plume).
