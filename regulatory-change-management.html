Platform Capabilities

regulatory intelligence,
automated.

From monitoring regulatory feeds to updating policies and notifying stakeholders, Uno handles the entire regulatory change lifecycle. AI agents continuously scan, analyze, and act on regulatory developments so your compliance team can focus on strategy, not surveillance.

01 Regulatory Feed Monitoring Continuous monitoring of 200+ regulatory sources including federal agencies, state regulators, international bodies, and industry-specific authorities. AI filters signal from noise, surfacing only the changes that matter to your organization based on industry, jurisdiction, and compliance profile. Monitoring
02 Impact Analysis & Mapping When a regulatory change is detected, AI automatically maps it to your existing controls, policies, and compliance obligations. Quantifies the impact - what needs to change, which teams are affected, and how urgent the response needs to be - all before your team opens the Federal Register. Analysis
03 Control Gap Identification Automatically identify gaps between new regulatory requirements and your current control environment. AI compares incoming requirements against your control library, highlighting where you already comply, where controls need strengthening, and where entirely new controls are needed. Gap Analysis
04 Policy Update Automation When regulatory changes require policy updates, AI drafts revisions aligned with the new requirements. Maintains version control, tracks approval workflows, and ensures policy language accurately reflects the latest regulatory expectations - cutting policy update cycles from weeks to days. Automation
05 Stakeholder Notification Intelligent routing of regulatory change notifications to the right stakeholders. Legal, compliance, IT security, privacy, and business unit leads receive targeted alerts with contextualized summaries explaining what changed, why it matters, and what action is required of them. Alerts
06 Compliance Calendar Management Centralized calendar tracking all regulatory deadlines, comment periods, implementation dates, and reporting requirements. AI proactively alerts on upcoming deadlines, tracks progress against milestones, and ensures nothing slips through the cracks across overlapping regulatory timelines. Calendar

Proactive Compliance

from reactive
to proactive.

01

Intelligent Change Detection

AI distinguishes between material regulatory changes and routine administrative updates. Natural language processing understands regulatory intent, not just text differences, so your team focuses on changes that genuinely impact compliance posture.

02

Cross-Regulation Impact Mapping

Understand how a single regulatory change ripples across multiple compliance frameworks. When NYDFS updates cybersecurity requirements, instantly see the impact on your SOC 2 controls, ISO 27001 compliance, and internal security policies simultaneously.

03

Automated Compliance Roadmaps

AI generates implementation roadmaps for regulatory changes, breaking down complex requirements into actionable workstreams with owners, timelines, and dependencies. Turn a 200-page final rule into a structured compliance project in minutes.

04

Regulatory Horizon Scanning

Look beyond published rules to track proposed regulations, comment periods, and enforcement trends. AI analyzes regulatory signals to predict upcoming requirements, giving your organization months of lead time to prepare for what is coming next.
